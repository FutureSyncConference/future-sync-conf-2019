about

25th April 2019, 9:30am - 5pm
Plymouth University

Future Sync is a tech conference keeping you in-sync with the latest digital trends.
The 2019 event took place on 25th April at Plymouth University, where we were joined by an audience of 250 developers, designers and business leaders from around the South West for a day of talks, networking and an interactive exhibition.

track 1software development

Explore the latest trends in software development including immersive tech, web, native and hybrid apps and games.

sponsored by PLYMOUTH SOFTWARE explore track

track 2creative design

Dive into the design behind software and physical products as well as the coming trends in UX, UI and VUI.

sponsored by THE MOMENT explore track

track 3business innovation

Hear from entrepreneurs and business pioneers to find innovative ways of delivering increased value to your clients.

sponsored by DIGITAL PLYMOUTH explore track
